Birce Dar makes sculptures, installations, and mixed-media art works. By working predominantly with raw materials, she is interested in their response to both her intuitive manipulation of them, and more organic changes, such as the passing of time.

Her conceptual approach dwells on storytelling and its capacity for manipulation.
